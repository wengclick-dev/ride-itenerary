🏍 JARC Ride Itinerary

Day 1

  • 4:30 AM – Assembly / Call Time at Jiabong Park. Preparation and safety briefing.
  • 5:00 AM – Departure from Jiabong Park.
  • 7:00 AM – Breakfast stop at any Bulalo house or local karenderya.
  • 9:00 AM – Arrival at Agas-Agas Bridge. Sightseeing, photos, and short rest.
  • 11:30 AM – Arrival at Padre Burgos, Southern Leyte.
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch at Tangkaan Beach. Swimming and relaxation.
  • Overnight – Stay overnight at Tangkaan Beach.

Day 2

  • 5:00 AM – Wake up and prepare for the next ride.
  • 5:15 AM – Breakfast at Tangkaan Beach / nearby eatery.
  • 5:45 AM – Departure going to Mt. Caningag.
  • 7:00 AM – Arrival at Mt. Caningag. Sightseeing, photo opportunity, and short rest.
  • 9:00 AM – Start ride going back via Hinunangan–Silago Southern Leyte Coastal Route.
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch stop at a local karenderya in Hinunangan or Silago.
  • 1:00 PM – Continue ride straight home.
  • 4:00 PM – Estimated arrival at Jiabong.
